1

Login & Authentication

User enters the system. Identity is verified and role headers are established (Admin, Staff, or User).

2

Submission

Staff creates a new record by filling metadata and uploading files. Unique Tracking ID is generated.

3

Admin Review

The core decision point where the Admin chooses to Approve, Return for correction, or Decline the request.

4

Task Assignment

Upon approval, the system dispatches the record to the Assignee's portal and sends a notification alert.

5

Execution & Completion

The Assignee performs the work and marks it as finished. All stakeholders are then notified of the final status.

6

Archive & Audit

The workflow ends with the record stored in the history for analytics, reporting, and future audits.
