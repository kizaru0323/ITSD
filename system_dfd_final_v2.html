Submitting Data

  • Staff Entry: Subject, Priority, Office, and Category.
  • Uploads: Files sent during the submission process.
  • Admin Inputs: Approval decisions and feedback remarks.

System Logic

  • Process 2.0: Automatically makes a unique Tracking ID.
  • Process 3.0: Moves the status from Pending to Approved or Declined.
  • Process 4.0: Moves the status from Approved to Completed.

Saving Information

  • Communications Database: Where all task history is kept.
  • Logs Database: A history of everything that happens (Who did What).
  • Notifications Database: Keeps track of all messages sent to users.
